"Sugar Beach"

Maybe the best thing you would want to see and experience if you go to Sipalay City is the famous Sugar Beach that starting to get attention not just from local tourist but foreign tourists as well. It is not so easily accessible because the road is pretty far so on foot would take some 10-15 mins of walking. But another way is by a boat rentable from the metropolitan center of the city. The sugary colored sand is fantastic and and if you can see from the photo it has scenic view of the distant sea and the almost quiet surroundings disturbed by the small waves and few tourists enjoying the experience. I really love our 3 days visit here and I love the view at the evening.

"Tinagong Dagat"

This view is from the top of one of the hills and rocky islets that surrounds a cove that called Tinagong Dagat which means a Hidden Sea in its english translation because its like a secret bay secluded by nature. The water here is calmer than compared to the sea waves that hitting the beaches. Before you can reach the part that I have taken the photo you have to cross a wooden bridge that is also of the highlights of visiting this hidden gem.

This pictures was taken when we visited Tinagong Dagat. Such a wonderful experience as we crossed the wooden bridge and then hiked into the hills. It was worth it when you are the top and you can see the beautiful view from up there.

"Foods, Drinks and A lot of People"

This shot I have taken is alongside the middle of a few kilometers of crowded by both locals and tourists. Most are walking around, sightseeing every corners for foods, clothes, pasalubongs, and different events. Some are in the sides, already drinking and eating some of the famous local foods especially the chicken inasal. Different stages are prepared before the night strikes, and some will introduce some events and the others will have bands to rock the party. It was loud all night, and it was for three days.

"Bands"

For me that love going for free concerts, this is one of the highlights of the festival. The first band that I know and played in the stage is Gracenote. This is the first time I have seen them in close because I only listen to them in YouTube so it is a wonderful experience. After three or four songs the band said goodbye to the crowd and because they are the last play I have to go to a different stage. There is also some bands that I know but I already forgotten what's their names. At almost midnight, another famous band have played and it is Imago. I super love that almost all people have stopped what their doing and listened to the band. It is also the first time I watched them in so close.
